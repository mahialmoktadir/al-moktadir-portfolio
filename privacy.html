Privacy & Security Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

AL-MOKTADIR is committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the security of any personal information you entrust to us. This Privacy & Security Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or utilize our services. We take your privacy seriously and strive to be transparent about our data practices.

2. Information Collected

We may collect the following types of information when you interact with our website or services:

Full Name
Email Address
Contact Information
Information submitted via contact forms
Basic technical data necessary for website functionality (browser type, device information, analytical metrics)

3. How Information Is Used

The information we collect is used solely for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Responding to inquiries, proposals, and service requests submitted through our contact forms.
  • Communicating with clients regarding project progress, deliverables, and business-related updates.
  • Improving website functionality, user experience, and content relevance based on aggregated usage data.
  • Providing customer support and resolving technical or service-related issues.

4. Data Protection

We implement reasonable and appropriate technical, administrative, and physical security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include secure data storage, encrypted communications where applicable, and restricted access to personal data on a need-to-know basis. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Cookies & Browser Storage

This website may use essential cookies and browser local storage features to enhance user experience and remember user preferences, such as theme mode (light/dark) selections. These storage mechanisms do not track your browsing activity across external websites. You may choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect certain functionality of the website.

6. Third-Party Services

AL-MOKTADIR does not unnecessarily integrate or share data with third-party services. Our website is built to operate independently with minimal external dependencies. If any third-party services become necessary for functionality or analytics in the future, users will be informed and provided with relevant details about how their data may be handled by such services.

7. User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You may request access to any personal information we hold about you that was submitted through our website or during project engagements.
  • Correction: You may request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete information we have about you.
  • Deletion: You may request that we delete your personal information where applicable, subject to any legal obligations requiring data retention.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in Section 10 below.

8. Children's Privacy

The services and content provided by AL-MOKTADIR are not intentionally directed toward or designed to attract individuals under the age required by applicable law. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that a minor has provided us with personal information without appropriate consent, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

9. Policy Updates

This Privacy & Security Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our data practices, legal requirements, or website functionality. When material changes are made, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised. We encourage users to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
